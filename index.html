Fabric types

Our current catalog, grouped by material.

Cotton

Combed and carded, 140–220 gsm. Best for shirts, dresses, everyday wear.

Linen

Breathable, textured, 180–230 gsm. Best for loose, warm-climate garments.

Denim

10–14oz, rigid or stretch. Best for jackets, trousers, structured pieces.

Satin

Smooth drape, 90–140 gsm. Best for eveningwear and linings.

Rayon

Soft fall, 120–160 gsm. Best for flowy dresses and blouses.
